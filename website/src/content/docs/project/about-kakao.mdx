Kakao Corporation (카카오) is a major internet platform company in Korea. [KakaoTalk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KakaoTalk), its flagship messaging app, serves tens of millions of users. The company operates services across messaging, commerce, AI, and more.

Actionbase was developed at Kakao to handle user interactions at this scale—likes, views, follows—across multiple services. It is now open source.
