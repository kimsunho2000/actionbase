Kakao is a leading South Korean internet and mobile platform company, operating major services including messaging, e-commerce, and payment platforms with tens of millions of users.

## Overview

Kakao is one of South Korea's largest technology companies, providing a wide range of digital services that are integral to daily life in South Korea. The company operates several major platforms that serve tens of millions of users.

## Major Services

### KakaoTalk

KakaoTalk is South Korea's dominant messaging platform, used by the vast majority of the country's smartphone users. It provides messaging, voice and video calls, and various integrated services.

### KakaoCommerce

KakaoCommerce operates e-commerce platforms including KakaoShopping, providing online shopping experiences integrated with Kakao's ecosystem.

### Other Services

Kakao operates numerous other services including:
- Payment platforms
- Mobility services
- Content platforms
- Enterprise solutions

## Actionbase in Production

Actionbase powers several major Kakao services, processing real-time user activity data for tens of millions of users. This production deployment demonstrates Actionbase's ability to handle large-scale, high-traffic workloads in a demanding production environment.
